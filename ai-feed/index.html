On June 21, Getty Images announced a multi-year display agreement with OpenAI that will surface over 400 million licensed assets directly inside ChatGPT search results, sending Getty stock up more than 123%.   • ChatGPT gains visual richness in search and discovery while Getty gains distribution to hundreds of millions of users - a mutual-benefit structure that sidesteps the zero-sum framing of most AI copyright disputes and could accelerate deals with news archives and stock media libraries
