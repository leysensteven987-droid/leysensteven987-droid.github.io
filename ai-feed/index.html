Unveiled June 24, Jalapeño is OpenAI's first custom ASIC chip, co-designed with Broadcom, targeting a 50% inference cost reduction versus third-party Nvidia GPUs - a critical move for a company that reportedly spent $14 billion serving ChatGPT on rented compute in 2025. What's moving

  • OpenAI used its own AI models to compress a chip design cycle that typically takes 18-24 months into just 9 months - demonstrating the AI-accelerates-AI feedback loop at commercial hardware scale for the first time
  • Owning silicon lets OpenAI co-optimize model architecture and hardware together, a capability GPU renters cannot match; if the 50% cost reduction materializes, OpenAI could reinvest the savings into pricing pressure on rivals or into higher-capability inference modes
